Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Our application only retrieves public tweets from a specific Twitter channel and does not collect any personal user information.

2. Data Usage

The collected tweets are used solely for transmission via n8n to other platforms defined by the user.

3. Data Sharing

No collected data is sold or shared with third parties. The information is used strictly for the operation of the service.

4. Security

We implement security measures to protect the data, but we cannot guarantee absolute protection against all security vulnerabilities.

5. User Rights

If you wish for us to stop accessing tweets from a specific channel, you can contact us to disable the service.

6. Modifications

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be displayed on our website.
